Need Help?

If you’re having trouble registering email us at Info@5KFoamFest.ca and let us know what problems you are encountering. Our staff will be more than happy to help you out!

CAN I TRANSFER TO A DIFFERENT LOCATION?

To transfer your registration to another location you will need to contact us at Info@5KFoamFest.ca there will be a $15 fee for each registration to switch races. You cannot transfer your registration to another race season (later year) and you must contact us BEFORE the race you are registered for the transfer to take place.

I DIDN’T RECEIVE MY REGISTRATION CONFIRMATION

If you did not receive your registration confirmation please email us at Info@5KFoamFest.ca and we will take care of you no problemo!

CAN MY CHILD DO THE RACE?

All children must be physically, mentally, and emotionally capable to complete the course with proper racing etiquette. Please see the age guidelines below: Ages 7 and under will not be able to participate in the 5k but we would love to have them join us for our pre and post race activities such as our Foam Pit and Bounce House! Ages 8-12 must run with an adult chaperone at all times. Ages 13+ can run in the 5k race without an adult chaperone but must adhere to all race rules and regulations and have a parent or guardian sign off on their waiver.

REFUNDS

The Foam Fest does NOT issue refunds. Regardless of the circumstances, we do not issue refunds. Make sure to check your calendar BEFORE signing up. You will not receive a refund if you cannot make the race or an unforeseeable circumstance is to happen. In the event of a date or venue change, we will make sure to notify you well in advance to make the necessary accommodations for your fun foam filled day! While we cannot guarantee the weather, we do aim for the best days possible. In the event of a lightening storm (yes, crash bang boom kind of deal), the event will unfortunately be cancelled. This is a very small possibility so need not fret!
